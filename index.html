An Economy On Wheels

Americas advanced system of roads help move people and goods everywhere from the east to the west. You can get to almost anywhere in america by using a road. Because of this, we are more connected.

TRAFFIC!!!

America needs to move, and sometime a little to much. Many of our roadways are crowded with lots of cars and trucks. Sometimes we try to solve this problem by adding more lanes, but all that does is add more space for more traffic.

Potholes

All this traffic leads to a lot of damage done to our roadways. One form of damage is the pothole. Potholes can be anything from a minor inconvenience to a serious danger! Many people hit potholes and die immediately. Others damage there vehicles. Many citys try to fix potholes on there roadways just to have more appear in a few years of the roads being used.
